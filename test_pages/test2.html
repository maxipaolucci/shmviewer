Event Coverage: Angelopolis Outlaw

Down in Colombia, a few dozen locals gathered together for an Outlaw Race on a narrow ridge run on the outskirts of Medellin. No prize money, no free gear, just traffic, narrow runs, and bragging rights. In the end S1 and Valhalla‘s Team of Bad Bitches swept the podium.

1st Place: Francis Cooper Darquea Mauro (Valhalla’s, S1, Caliber, Sucrose)

2nd Place: Santiago Granada (Valhalla’s, S1)

3rd Place: Juan Pablo Villegas (Gullwing, S1)

4th Place: Felipe Marin (Valhalla’s, S1)
